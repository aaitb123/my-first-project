Aitbekova Group

We develop heterogeneous catalytic processes by synergistically coupling spatiotemporally isolated photo- and thermocatalytic steps. We seek to create efficient and sustainable alternatives for fuel and chemical production.

Our approach spans multiple scales, from developing catalytic materials at the nanoscale to engineering reactors at the macroscale. Areas of interest include hydrocarbon chemistry, selective oxidations, and biomass valorization. Equipped with a toolkit of catalyst design and synthesis, in-situ/operando characterization, microkinetic modeling, and reactor engineering, our interdisciplinary team strives to bring innovations to the chemical industry.

Research

Our research efforts aim to develop innovative and energy-efficient processes to generate sustainable fuels and chemicals. Through a fundamental understanding of coupled photo-/thermochemical processes, we seek to find synergy between photochemical and thermochemical effects. We will apply this knowledge to engineer innovative materials and processes that outperform traditional processes.
